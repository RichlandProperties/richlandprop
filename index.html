Our Story

From Distressed Assets
to Dream Homes

At Richland Property Partners, we believe experience matters — especially in a market as dynamic and diverse as Southern and Central California. Every client comes to us with a unique set of goals, whether they're searching for a home, building an investment portfolio, or navigating the complexities of selling a real estate–owned property. Understanding those needs and tailoring our approach accordingly is at the heart of what we do.

Our journey has taken us through nearly every facet of real estate. We've acquired and repositioned distressed properties, represented buyers and sellers across a wide range of transactions, and partnered with lenders to manage and dispose of REO assets. These firsthand experiences have given us a deep, practical understanding of the challenges and nuances that come with each type of transaction — insight that allows us to guide our clients with clarity, efficiency, and confidence.

Today, Richland Property Partners operates as a full-service brokerage dedicated to serving buyers, sellers, investors, and lenders throughout Southern and Central California. Our philosophy is simple: boutique-level attention, seasoned expertise, and an unwavering commitment to integrity, transparency, and trust. Every transaction, every client, every time.

Jose Gutierrez is the Broker and Founder of Richland Property Partners. A graduate of UCLA with a degree in International Economics and a veteran of the U.S. Army Reserve, José entered the real estate industry in 2003 with a focus on distressed fix‑and‑flip properties throughout Central California. Over the past two decades, he has completed more than 2,850 residential transactions and acquired and disposed of over $85 million in distressed assets. His background spans brokerage, investment, asset management, and complex distressed‑asset strategy — experience that gives his clients a level of insight few brokers can offer. Fully bilingual in English and Spanish, Jose leads with integrity, clarity, and a deep commitment to helping every client achieve their real estate goals.
