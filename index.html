Our Story

From Distressed Assets
to Dream Homes

Richland Property Partners was founded by Jose Gutierrez, a UCLA-educated economist and U.S. Army Reserve veteran who began investing in distressed properties in Central California in 2003. What started as a hands-on fix-and-flip operation grew into one of the region's most experienced real estate firms — with over 2,700 residential transactions completed across California.

Today, Richland Property Partners operates as a full-service brokerage serving buyers, sellers, and investors throughout Southern and Central California. Our approach is simple: boutique attention, deep expertise, and an unwavering commitment to integrity, transparency, and trust — on every transaction, every time.

Jose Gutierrez

Jose Gutierrez is the Broker and Founder of Richland Property Partners. A UCLA graduate in International Economics and U.S. Army Reserve veteran, Jose began his real estate career in 2003 — specializing in distressed fix-and-flip properties across Central California. Over two decades he has completed more than 2,700 residential transactions and acquired over $25 million in distressed mortgage assets through his note-buying firm, River Flow Funding. Bilingual in English and Spanish, Jose leads with integrity and a deep commitment to helping every client achieve their real estate goals.
